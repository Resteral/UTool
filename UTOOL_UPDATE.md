# UTool Brand & Feature Update Checklist

Welcome to **UTool** (formerly BuildFlow) — your premium Construction Job, Crew & Billing Manager! We have updated the platform to completely match your specifications:

## 1. 🏷️ Full UTool Renaming
- Updated site title to `UTool - Premium Construction Job & Invoice Manager`.
- Modernized the brand logo elements from `BF` to `UT` and updated all navigation & login references to **UTool**.
- Customized user login overlays and digital contracts to present the official **UTool** developer seal.

## 2. 🧹 Empty Sandbox Slates
- Redefined standard seed data to start as a pristine empty workspace on initial boot.
- Preserved the ability to load Springfield Villa and Downtown Plaza demo project sheets at any time using the new **Load Mock Demo Data** sandbox trigger.

## 3. 📊 Registered Units Tracking
We converted standard monetary stats card metrics into **Registered Core Entity Counts** that update dynamically in real time:
- **Registered Projects**: Overall count of active construction site sheets.
- **Crew Workforce**: Registered operational workforce units.
- **Supplies Catalog**: Count of registered items in the custom material catalog.
- **Drafted Invoices**: Amount of invoices & quotes generated.

## 4. 🤝 Certified Partners Dynamic Sync
- Implemented Supabase table hooks on the public `buildflow_partners` table.
- Synchronized all uploaded contractor branding logos under their respective IDs so that **all visiting customers and guests see the certified partner banner**, not just the contractor who uploaded it.
